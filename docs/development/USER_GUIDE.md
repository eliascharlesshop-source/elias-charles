# User Guide

Welcome to EC Store! This guide will help you navigate and use all the features of our e-commerce platform.

## 🎯 Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Click **Sign Up** in the top navigation
2. Fill in your information:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First and last name
3. Click **Create Account**
4. You'll be automatically logged in

### Logging In
1. Click **Log In** in the top navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

## 🛍️ Shopping Experience

### Browsing Products

#### Homepage
- **Featured Products**: Curated selection on the homepage
- **Collections**: Browse by category (Apparel, Boards, Self-Care, etc.)
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific items

#### Product Pages
Each product page includes:
- **High-quality images**: Click to zoom
- **Detailed description**: Product features and specifications
- **Pricing**: Regular price and sale price (if applicable)
- **Size/variant selection**: Choose your preferred options
- **Stock status**: Availability information
- **Add to Cart**: Quick add button

#### Product Collections
- **Men's Collection**: Curated items for men
- **Women's Collection**: Fashion and lifestyle items for women
- **Apparel**: Clothing and accessories
- **Boards**: Skateboards, surfboards, and gear
- **Self-Care**: Wellness and beauty products
- **Life**: Lifestyle and home items
- **Sale**: Discounted items

### Search and Filtering

#### Search Functionality
- **Global Search**: Use the search bar in the header
- **Search by**: Product name, description, category, or tags
- **Search Results**: Displays matching products with thumbnails

#### Filter Options
- **Category**: Filter by product type
- **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum price
- **Availability**: Show only in-stock items
- **Featured Items**: View highlighted products
- **Sort Options**:
  - Price: Low to High / High to Low
  - Name: A-Z / Z-A
  - Newest First
  - Featured First

## 🛒 Shopping Cart

### Adding Items
1. Navigate to a product page
2. Select size/variant if applicable
3. Choose quantity
4. Click **Add to Cart**
5. Item appears in cart (top right icon)

### Managing Your Cart
- **View Cart**: Click the cart icon in the header
- **Update Quantity**: Use +/- buttons or type amount
- **Remove Items**: Click the trash icon
- **Clear Cart**: Remove all items at once

### Cart Information
Your cart displays:
- **Item Details**: Product name, image, price, quantity
- **Subtotal**: Total before taxes and shipping
- **Item Count**: Number of items in cart
- **Quick Actions**: Update, remove, or continue shopping

## 💳 Checkout Process

### Step 1: Review Cart
- Verify all items and quantities
- Apply any promo codes
- Review pricing breakdown

### Step 2: Shipping Information
Fill in your shipping address:
- **First and Last Name**
- **Street Address**
- **City, State, ZIP Code**
- **Country**
- **Phone Number** (optional)

### Step 3: Billing Information
- **Same as Shipping**: Check if addresses match
- **Different Billing Address**: Enter separate billing details

### Step 4: Shipping Method
Choose your preferred shipping option:
- **Standard Shipping** (5-7 business days): $10
- **Express Shipping** (2-3 business days): $20
- **Overnight Shipping** (1 business day): $35
- **Free Shipping**: On orders over $75

### Step 5: Payment
Select your payment method:
- **Credit/Debit Card**: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
- **PayPal**: Secure PayPal checkout
- **Apple Pay**: For supported devices

#### Payment Information
- **Card Number**: 16-digit card number
- **Expiry Date**: MM/YY format
- **Security Code**: CVV on back of card
- **Cardholder Name**: Name as it appears on card

### Step 6: Order Review
- **Review all details** before placing order
- **Terms and Conditions**: Accept before proceeding
- **Place Order**: Complete your purchase

## 📦 Order Management

### Order Confirmation
After placing an order, you'll receive:
- **Order Confirmation Email**: Immediate receipt
- **Order Number**: For tracking and reference
- **Estimated Delivery**: Expected arrival date

### Order Status
Track your order through these stages:
1. **Order Placed**: Payment confirmed
2. **Processing**: Order being prepared
3. **Shipped**: Order dispatched
4. **Out for Delivery**: With delivery carrier
5. **Delivered**: Order completed

### Order Tracking
- **Email Notifications**: Updates sent to your email
- **Account Dashboard**: View in your profile
- **Tracking Number**: Use with carrier's website
