# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 🛍️ Shopping Questions

### How do I place an order?
1. Browse products and add items to your cart
2. Click the cart icon to review your items
3. Proceed to checkout and fill in shipping information
4. Select a shipping method and enter payment details
5. Review your order and click "Place Order"

### Can I modify or cancel my order?
- **Before Processing**: Contact us immediately to modify or cancel
- **After Processing**: Orders cannot be modified, but you can return items
- **Contact**: Email us at orders@ecstore.com with your order number

### Do I need an account to shop?
- **Guest Checkout**: Available for one-time purchases
- **Account Benefits**: Order tracking, faster checkout, order history
- **Recommendation**: Create an account for the best experience

### How do I apply a promo code?
1. Add items to your cart
2. Go to checkout
3. Look for "Promo Code" or "Discount Code" field
4. Enter your code and click "Apply"
5. Discount will be reflected in your total

## 📦 Shipping Questions

### What shipping options are available?
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days ($10)
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days ($20)
- **Overnight Shipping**: 1 business day ($35)
- **Free Shipping**: Orders over $75 (standard shipping)

### How can I track my order?
1. Check your email for tracking number
2. Visit the carrier's website (UPS, FedEx, USPS)
3. Enter your tracking number
4. View real-time updates

### Do you ship internationally?
- **Currently**: Limited international shipping
- **Available Countries**: Canada, UK, Australia
- **Shipping Costs**: Calculated at checkout
- **Customs**: Customer responsible for duties and taxes

### When will my order ship?
- **Processing Time**: 1-2 business days
- **Shipping Days**: Monday through Friday
- **Holidays**: No processing on federal holidays
- **Tracking**: Email sent when order ships

## 💳 Payment Questions

### What payment methods do you accept?
- **Credit Cards**: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- **Digital Wallets**: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Security**: All payments processed securely
- **No Fees**: No additional processing fees

### Is my payment information secure?
- **SSL Encryption**: All transactions encrypted
- **PCI Compliance**: Meeting security standards
- **No Storage**: We don't store full credit card numbers
- **Trusted Partners**: Using secure payment processors

### Why was my payment declined?
Common reasons:
- **Insufficient Funds**: Check account balance
- **Billing Address**: Must match card address
- **Expired Card**: Check expiration date
- **Security Hold**: Contact your bank
- **Daily Limits**: Credit card spending limits

### Can I split payment between cards?
- **Currently**: Single payment method per order
- **Gift Cards**: Can be combined with other payment
- **Store Credit**: Can be applied to reduce total
- **Contact Us**: For special payment arrangements

## 🔄 Returns & Exchanges

### What is your return policy?
- **Return Window**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Condition**: Items must be unused, unworn, with tags
- **Original Packaging**: Required for returns
- **Refund Processing**: 5-7 business days after we receive item

### How do I return an item?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Order History
3. Select the order and item to return
4. Print the prepaid return label
5. Package item and drop off at specified carrier

### What items cannot be returned?
- **Final Sale Items**: Marked as final sale
- **Personalized Items**: Custom or engraved products
- **Intimate Apparel**: For hygiene reasons
- **Used Items**: Items showing wear or damage

### How long do refunds take?
- **Processing**: 1-2 business days after we receive return
- **Bank Processing**: 3-5 business days for credit cards
- **PayPal**: 1-2 business days
- **Store Credit**: Immediate (if selected)

### Can I exchange instead of return?
- **Size Exchanges**: Available for clothing
- **Color/Style**: Subject to availability
- **Price Differences**: You pay/receive difference
- **Process**: Contact customer service first

## 👤 Account Questions

### How do I create an account?
1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Fill in your name and other details
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Verify your email if prompted

### I forgot my password. What do I do?
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Follow instructions to create new password

### How do I update my account information?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to "Account Settings" or "Profile"
3. Update the information you want to change
4. Click "Save Changes"
5. Verify changes if prompted

### Can I delete my account?
- **Yes**: Contact customer service to delete account
- **Data Removal**: Personal information will be removed
- **Order History**: May be retained for business records
- **Email**: Send request to privacy@ecstore.com

## 🚚 Delivery Questions

### What if I'm not home for delivery?
- **Carrier Policies**: Each carrier has different procedures
- **Delivery Attempts**: Usually 2-3 attempts made
- **Hold for Pickup**: Available at local facilities
- **Delivery Instructions**: Add special instructions at checkout

### My package was marked delivered but I didn't receive it
1. Check around your property (porches, bushes, etc.)
2. Ask neighbors or building management
3. Check if someone else accepted delivery
4. Wait 24 hours (sometimes scanning errors occur)
5. Contact us if still missing

### Can I change my delivery address?
- **Before Shipping**: Contact us immediately
- **After Shipping**: Contact the carrier directly
- **Intercept Service**: May be available for a fee
- **Undeliverable**: Package will return to us

### Do you require a signature for delivery?
- **Standard Orders**: Signature not required
- **High-Value Orders**: May require signature ($200+)
- **Request Signature**: Available upon request
- **Safe Delivery**: Carriers attempt secure delivery
